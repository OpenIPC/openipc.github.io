## What Is Faceter
Faceter's video surveillance solution combines three layers: hardware, cloud, and analytics.

### Hardware
A lineup of OpenIPC and Faceter cameras: models for everyday tasks and professional-grade devices for sites with higher requirements.
Every model works with the Faceter app right out of the box — no extra NVR, no manual RTSP stream setup. Third-party cameras are also supported via RTSP and ONVIF.

### Cloud
The platform stores footage archives and provides remote access through a web interface, mobile apps (iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, Android TV), and a Telegram bot. Hybrid setups are supported too, with part of the footage stored locally on an NVR and part in the cloud.

### Analytics
This is the core of the product: the system detects motion and describes what's happening in plain, human language. Instead of "Motion detected in Zone 2" users get reports like "A man in a dark jacket crossed the perimeter near the gate" That's the Faceter AI agent at work.
