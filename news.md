## Aug 17, 2025

**OpenIPC Project Joins [Linux Foundation][linux-foundation] as [Associate Member][associate-member].**

We are pleased to announce that the international [OpenIPC][github] project has officially joined
the [Linux Foundation][linux-foundation] as an [Associate Member][associate-member]. We extend our
sincere congratulations to all participants and colleagues who have contributed to this significant
milestone.
